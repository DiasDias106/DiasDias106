# About Me:
I am a third-year Computer Engineering student, and technology has been my great passion since I was a child. Fascinated by understanding how things work and creating innovative solutions, I enthusiastically explore different areas, from Backend and Frontend to Networking and Cybersecurity. I am constantly learning new skills and applying them to realistic and challenging projects that allow me to grow both technically and personally. I believe technology has the power to transform the world, and my goal is to one day make a difference through what I create and contribute. I enjoy taking on new challenges, stepping out of my comfort zone, and seizing every opportunity to evolve as a professional and as a person.
